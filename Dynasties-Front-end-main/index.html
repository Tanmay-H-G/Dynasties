Dynasty Timeline

300 BCE Maurya Empire Founded
Chandragupta Maurya unites northern India; Ashoka’s reign (268–232 BCE) promotes Buddhism
4th Century CE Gupta Empire Peaks
Chandragupta I and Samudragupta; Golden Age with advancements in science, art, and literature
9th Century CE Chola Dynasty Rises
Rajaraja Chola I (985–1014) expands naval power; builds Brihadeeswarar Temple
1206 Delhi Sultanate Established
Qutb-ud-din Aibak founds dynasty; Qutub Minar construction begins
1336 Vijayanagara Empire Founded
Harihara I and Bukka Raya I establish; Hampi becomes cultural center
1347 Bahmani Sultanate Formed
Ala-ud-Din Bahman Shah establishes Deccan Islamic rule
1526 Mughal Empire Begins
Babur defeats Ibrahim Lodi at Panipat; Akbar’s reign (1556–1605) marks peak
1674 Maratha Empire Founded
Shivaji Maharaj crowned; expands under Peshwas
1799 Sikh Empire Consolidated
Maharaja Ranjit Singh unites Punjab; Golden Temple enhanced
1858 British Raj Established
Post-1857 revolt; Delhi becomes capital in 1911
1947 Indian Independence
End of British Raj; India and Pakistan emerge
